Help

Welcome to the Expense Tracker Help page. Here you can find information on how to use the application.

  • Adding an Account: Use the form on the homepage to add a new account.
  • Deleting an Account: Click the delete button next to an account to remove it.
  • Adding a Transaction: Use the form on the homepage to add a new transaction.
  • Viewing Transactions: Click on the "Transactions" link to view all transactions.
  • Updating a Transaction: Click the update button next to a transaction to edit it.
  • Deleting a Transaction: Click the delete button next to a transaction to remove it.
  • Summary: Click on the "Summary" link to view a summary of your expenses and account balances.
